## 🎯 Detailed Comparison

### Vercel
**Best For**: Modern static sites, Next.js projects, performance

**Pros**:
- ✅ Extremely fast (Edge Network)
- ✅ Zero setup (connect GitHub)
- ✅ Built-in analytics
- ✅ Preview deployments
- ✅ Excellent performance
- ✅ Great DX
- ✅ Generous free tier
- ✅ Excellent support

**Cons**:
- ❌ 100GB/month bandwidth limit
- ❌ Overkill for simple docs
- ❌ Vendor lock-in
- ❌ Less suitable for non-Next.js

**Free Tier**:
- Unlimited projects
- 100GB bandwidth/month
- Auto SSL
- Custom domain
- Preview deployments

**Best For**:
- Performance-critical
- Modern tech stacks
- Professional projects
- Best DX

**Cost**: $0 (free tier)
